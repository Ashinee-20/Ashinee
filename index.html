About Me

I am a B.Tech student specializing in Artificial Intelligence with a strong foundation in development and research. Over the past 1.5 years, I have completed three industrial internships, including two at Uber, where I worked on data analytics, backend development, and process automation. My experience also extends to computer vision and machine learning, showcased during my internship at GKN Aerospace.

I have published three research papers in prominent conferences like NAACL, ICITIIT, and ICISPD, highlighting my contributions to NLP, vehicle violation detection, and predictive modeling. Additionally, I lead as the Project Head for COMPCSOC IEEE and serve as a Placement Coordinator, balancing technical expertise with leadership and organizational skills. My passion lies in solving real-world problems through innovation and collaboration.
